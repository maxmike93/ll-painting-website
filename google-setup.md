# Google Setup

## Google Business Profile

1. Go to [Google Business Profile](https://www.google.com/intl/en/business/).
2. Create or claim your Business Profile.
3. Make sure the profile matches your website exactly:
   - Business name: `L&L Painting`
   - Phone: `270-201-1447`
   - Website: your live website URL
   - Address: `4131 Clarks River Rd, Paducah, KY 42003`
4. Set the business as a service-area business if needed and define the service area to match your real coverage.
5. Add services:
   - Residential Painting
   - Commercial Painting
   - Epoxy Flooring
   - Powerwashing
   - Light Construction
6. Add your logo, cover photo, and real project photos.
7. Add your website URL and appointment/quote link if available.

## Google Search Console

1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/about).
2. Add your website as a property.
3. Best option: use a `Domain` property if you control your DNS.
4. Faster option: use `URL prefix` if you want to verify just the exact live site URL.
5. After verification, open `Sitemaps`.
6. Submit: `sitemap.xml`
