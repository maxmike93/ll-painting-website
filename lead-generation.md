# Lead Generation System

## Google Lead Flow

- Keep service pages live for residential painting, commercial painting, epoxy flooring, and powerwashing.
- Keep the service area page live and expand it over time with nearby town pages.
- Add real before-and-after photos to the gallery.
- Add real customer reviews to the reviews page and Google Business Profile.
- Submit the sitemap in Google Search Console.

## Facebook Lead Flow

- Post before-and-after projects weekly.
- Link ads and boosted posts to `estimate.html`.
- Use the Facebook page as a trust signal on every page.
- Respond fast with a short qualifying script.

## Marketplace Lead Flow

- Post separate listings for painting, epoxy flooring, and powerwashing.
- Reuse headlines from the service pages so the message stays consistent.
- Link customers to the estimate page or phone number.
