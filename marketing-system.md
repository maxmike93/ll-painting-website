**Tagline options**

- Painting Done Right the First Time

- Painting, Powerwashing, and Property Refresh Work

## Lead Sources

1. Website lead form and click-to-call at 270-201-1447
2. Google Business Profile
3. Facebook Page and Marketplace ads
4. Facebook lead ads
5. Referral and review requests

## Weekly Operating Rhythm

### Monday

- Post 3 Marketplace listings with before/after photos
- Follow up with all open estimates

### Tuesday

- Publish one before/after project on Facebook and Google Business Profile
- Text two recent customers asking for a review

### Wednesday

- Call unresponsive leads from the last 7 days
- Update gallery photos on the website

### Thursday

- Post a short paint-prep or transformation video
- Re-share a testimonial

### Friday

- Review ad performance
- Adjust ad creative or offer

### Saturday

- Confirm next week's estimate appointments
- Send "last chance" follow-up texts
